History of NordicTravels

NordicTravels was originally a Family-Run Skiing business in the 1930's, aiming to compete with the Austrian Skiing companies. They set up stands in all the big cities; Moscow, London, Paris, New York, Barcelona, Berlin, Bonn, Montreal, you name it! It was based in Uppsala, Sweden, and was recently purchased by the Nordic Co-Operation as an official tour representative of the Nordic Council. We get 85% of our funding directly from the European Union, and we only get about 15% of our funding through sales.

Our Swedish Tours (Best Sellers)

Gothenburg and Malmö (2 days)

Explore the beaches of Western Sweden, visit museums, enjoy fishing in the North Sea, and eat at 4.x stars Indian restaurants.

Småland/Kalmar (2 days)

Explore Kalmar's beaches, stay in a complex of beautiful cottages, fishing for those who would like, and excellent, first class pizza at Kalmar Pizza.

Stockholm and Uppsala (4 days)

Visit the beautiful capital of Sweden. SL card included. Dogs and Cats are welcome to join. The tour is free for children 11 and under. Have a good night's sleep at the Hilton Luxury Hotel in Rådhuset, as well as 4.x stars fine dining at Pagoda Sushiplats in Uppsala. This is our best selling package. We recommend that you buy 6 months in advance, as this tour only has 40 spots and become more pricey when less tickets are available!
