History of NordicTravels

NordicTravels was originally a Family-Run Skiing business in the 1930's, aiming to compete with the Austrian Skiing companies. They set up stands in all the big cities; Moscow, London, Paris, New York, Barcelona, Berlin, Bonn, Montreal, you name it! It was based in Uppsala, Sweden, and was recently purchased by the Nordic Co-Operation as an official tour representative of the Nordic Council. We get 85% of our funding directly from the European Union, and we only get about 15% of our funding through sales.

Our Norwegian Tours

Tromsø (4 days)

Explore the Arctic wonders of Norway. All visas, etc. is included. Eat at Wilhelmus Musique le France, a 5 star fine dining restaurant. Tips are appreciated.

Oslo (3 days)

Explore the all-electric capital Oslo, where 9/10 cars are electric! Eat at Italiano Pizzeria Mamma Mia Pasta to satisfy your cravings. IShowSpeed's mass stampede location will also be shown in this package.

Svalbard/Longyearbyen & Barentsburg (10 days)

Gaze at the International Seed Vault, and explore Soviet settlements. Flights are not included, and neither are restaurants as then the package would be way over our budget. Howecer, you could get good night's sleep at Cabin le Arctic Circle.

Bergen and Stavanger (4 days)

Explore the snowy beauty of Western Norway. Sleep at the ice hotel. If that is not for you, you can stay at the gorgeous cabins and cottages by the river. Norwegian Pølse for food. Pets are welcome (Cats & Dogs).
